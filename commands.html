Commands Prefix "-"
Play < url/name > Plays the given url/name
Stop Stops the music
Skip Skips the music
Pause Pauses the music
Resume Resumes the music
Lyrics Gives the lyrics for the song playing
Shuffle Shuffles the queue
Loop Loops the song playing
Queue Shows the servers music queue
Covid < country > Displays covid stats on the given location
Stats Shows the bots stats
